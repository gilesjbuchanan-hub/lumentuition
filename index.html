Why Lumen

We care. A lot.

As recent high school graduates, we at Lumen understand the stress and pressure that high school can bring, and the importance of a tutor in alleviating that. We aim to not just teach content, but to build the confidence and skills that carry students well beyond the classroom.

Tailored for the student, not the syllabus

In contrast to a fixed "one size fits all" lesson plan, we figure out how you learn and build every session around that.

Battle-tested tutors

Everyone at Lumen has been where you are, with recent experience and knowledge on how to achieve profound results.

Flexible, convenient scheduling

We operate around the clock, seven days a week, with an easy and flexible bookings system, and free 24 hour cancellations.

Noticeable progress with satisfying results

Our main focus is improvement, from simply solving a problem you were stuck on, all the way to achieving your ATAR goal.
